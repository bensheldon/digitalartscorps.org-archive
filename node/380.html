[CTCVISTA] IMPORTANT TAX INFO! Update your address on Corp site for W2s

Categories:
  • CTC VISTA Listserv

    ATTENTION All Current VISTAs
    and VISTA Alumni Who Served during the 2006 Tax Year!

    The Corporation for National and
    Community Service will send out the 2006 W2s and 1099s (education award
    users only) by the end of January. 

    To ensure we have your current
    mailing address, please be sure to log into the AmeriCorps Online
    Payment System at     http://www.americorps.gov/for_individuals/online/index.asp
     and update your permanent
    and current mailing addresses (IT DOESN'T MATTER WHAT YOU ENTERED IN THE CTCVISTA.ORG SITE - YOU STILL NEED TO UPDATE THIS on your own).  You can use this system even if you
    selected the cash stipend option and not the AmeriCorps education award.

    If you have not already created
    an account, you will need to complete a simple registration process by
    following the steps below:

      1. Access the system
        through the AmeriCorps.gov website;
      2. In the login box,
        click "Help/Register";
      3. On the next page,
        click the "Register to create a new Member/Alum account."  On the
        registration page, enter your last name, date of birth, social security
        number and current e-mail address;
      4. Click "Submit" and an
        e-mail will be sent to you which will include a link giving you access
        into the system.
      5. Once there, you will
        be asked to create a Username and Password.
      6. Click "Submit" and the
        system will bring up your personalized homepage.

    Once you have registered and
    created a username and password, you will always be able to log in to
    the system from the front page of the AmeriCorps website.  Once logged
    in, you can update your mailing addresses as needed.
