AmeriCorps
members are known for getting things done. Now you have a chance capture
those moments of service by participating in the

first-ever AmeriCorps video contest!

All you need is a video cam,
digital camera, or cell phone and your own creativity to make a 30-60 second
spot answering the question:

"Why
AmeriCorps?"

Entries must include the
AmeriCorps logo, and the videos should be informative, memorable, and – did
we mention creative?


Entries are due
June 1.  The contest is not limited to AmeriCorps members, but
participants must be over 18.

Go online and enter your video today!

  
 
 

www.AmeriCorpsContest.org

AmeriCorps engages
75,000 Americans each year in intensive service to meet community needs
in education, the environment, public safety, and other areas. AmeriCorps
is administered by the Corporation for National and Community Service,
which also oversees Senior Corps and Learn and Serve America.
