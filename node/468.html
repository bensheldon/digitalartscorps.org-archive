Freedom 2 Connect Conference March 5 & 6 @ Washington, DC

Categories:
  • CTC VISTA Listserv

Hey all,

I just got word that VISTAs can get a discounted rate ($150) to the
Freedom to Connect (F2C) Conference. The conference is taking place
in Washington, DC on March 5 & 6. If you can only make it to one
conference this year, I would still recommend attending NTEN, but if
you're in the area, or find this stuff really interesting, it would be
worth it to go as well.

http://freedom-to-connect.net/

F2C is a meeting of people engaged with network connectivity and
applications as vendors, customers, regulators, legislators, citizens
and co-creators. This year, the theme of F2C is how networks are
changing our fundamental economic and social assumptions.

WHAT: F2C is a two-day meeting where the builders and users of the
networked economy engage communications policy makers, network
technologists, application creators, and the builders and operators of
the new infrastructure.
