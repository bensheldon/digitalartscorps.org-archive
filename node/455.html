Finding Conference Roommates

Categories:
  • CTC VISTA Listserv

Hi all,

To follow-up on Paul's previous email concerning attending the NTEN conference:

VISTAs planning to attend should have your organization reserve a hotel room ASAP
as they quickly fill up--but can also be canceled at the last minute (usually 24-48 hours).  In order to save money the VISTA Project encourages VISTAs to room with another VISTA.



1) Reserve a hotel room.

2) Find a roommate

3) One person cancels their reservation

We have set up an editable page of people requiring roommates.  Click the "Edit" tab below the title and add yourself to the list:


http://www.ctcvista.org/node/454

Then contact other people on the list and figure things out.  Once you've found a roommate, please remove yourself from the list.
