[CTCVISTA] Bill Moyers on America "The Net at Risk" airing this week

Categories:
  • CTC VISTA Listserv

For those of you with TV (and possibly cable), this looks mighty interesting...



The Net At Risk (From OPB schedule) Episode: #103, 86 minutes




Explores efforts by big corporations to turn the Internet into a toll road by lobbying Washington.  TV Wednesday, October 18, 2006 at 9:00 PM



TV Friday, October 20, 2006 at 3:00 AM







http://www.pbs.org/moyers/moyersonamerica/net/neutrality.html








Moyers on America presents a new, serious investigative
analysis of the future of broadband, the Net and media --- from
municipalities trying to Wifi or rewire their cities, to the large
phone companies who claim that they 'own-the net' or large media
concerns who have the power to take control of the information and
stories you see and hear.
