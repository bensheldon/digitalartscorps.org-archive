11 - 12 Months

Categories:
  • adjourning
  • end of service

During the last 2 months of DASCorps service VISTAs start realizing that their end of service is near and they need to start preparing for their departure.

Some typical VISTA behaviors and attitudes:
- Negativity
- Dissatisfaction
- Hostility
- Purging
- Crisis

Some general tips for VISTAs during this time:
- Continue to think about and prepare for your future
- Build bridges - goodbye doesn't have to mean forever
- Give staff suggestions on how things could be done even better next year
- Prepare documentation of all your projects to leave with your host organization and to give to the Transmission Project
