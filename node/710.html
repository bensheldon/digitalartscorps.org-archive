vote for us on NetSquared, be a GRUT presenter, and next CTC VISTA round due 04/13

Categories:
  • CTC VISTA Listserv

Hi all,

Three quick things:

1. We're nominated for a NetSquared Innovation Award.  But you must vote for us (yes, sort of like American Idol without the signing).  You must register as a
NetSquared.org user (it's ok, it's a fun site to be registered on) and go to http://www.netsquared.org/projects/proposals
 and search for "The CTC VISTA Project".  Votes must be in by Friday April 14th!

2. We're partnering with the Organizer's Collaborative to put on the
GrassRoots Use of Technology Conference <http://organizerscollaborative.org/conference> this June (21-23 in Lowell MA) and we'd love to have CTC VISTAs come
present.  They're looking for workshops presenters on these subjects (or you can suggest your own):

  • Getting a basic website up and running in less
    than 2 hours
  • Security Issues
  • Online Fundraising strategies & tools

  • Creating effective distance learning
    opportunities on the cheap

Email Ben/me if you're interested.

3. A quick reminder that the organizational Letters of Intent (the first step in the CTC VISTA organizational application process
) is due THIS FRIDAY April 13th.  See <http://ctcvista.org/guidelines> for details.

-Danielle
