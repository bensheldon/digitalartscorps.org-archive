100 Second Film Festival - CALL FOR ENTRIES 2007 !!!

Calling all video and film makers, young and old, amateur and professional, artist, activist, hobbyist or dilettante...

Lowell Telecommunications Corporation announces a worldwide

Call for Entries

for the third annual 100 SECOND FILM FESTIVAL.   The 100 SECOND FILM FESTIVAL is a collaborative remixable film festival
bringing people together around creating, sharing and watching videos
that are one hundred seconds or less in duration.

The deadline
for
entries is July 1st


This festival is powered by us, people with a camera and an idea who
want a platform, and who want to connect with a bigger collective
experience.


Anybody can create a 100 second piece. There is specifically no theme
or subject, no required entrance fee, the only constraint is time.

Contribute your video. Make 2007 the best festival yet.

Complete details are on the website

http://100second.ltc.org.

The entire festival is published to the web under a Creative Commons License (

http://www.creativecommons.org
).

Submissions can be entered online directly via Blip.TV (http://blip.tv) or the Internet Archive (

http://www.archive.org) with a tag of
"100seconds"
The festival is viewable on the Democracy (http://www.getdemocracy.com) open source video player.



Submissions can also be sent to
100 SECOND FILM FESTIVAL
c/o LTC
246 Market St,
Lowell, MA 01852
USA

Please label all
entries with title, credits, a two sentence description and contact information.

The 2007 festival will premiere in Lowell at the Revolving Museum (

http://www.revolvingmuseum.org) October 6.
