Attached are three worksheets used during the 2010 PSO.

  • Skills Inventory Worksheet: A worksheet to help you determine what skills you have and what skills you need while looking at your VAD.
  • Consensus Workshop: A sheet detailing the Open-Space facilitation process we went through on the last day of PSO.
