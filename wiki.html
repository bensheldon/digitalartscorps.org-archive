Digital Arts Corps Wiki

Welcome to the Digital Arts Service Corps' shared wiki space.

This wiki is split into 3 sections:

1. The 1 Year Curriculum- This is an overview of a typical DASCorps year with resources and wiki articles specific to time during your year of service.

2. Benefits & Living on the Stipend- This area has resources and information about food stamps, rental assistance, health assistance, student loan issues, etc. etc.

3. General Resources- This area was created to encourage a free exchange of ideas and resources among Digital Arts Service Corps members, participating organizations, and others working in the field of community media and technology.

Please add to the wiki as you see fit!

If you're a Member, log into the site and wiki away.
